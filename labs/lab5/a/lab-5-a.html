Tortellini with Alfredo Sauce

Tortellini with alfredo sauce is a fairly simple dish, but it is one that I enjoy.
My father has made it using the same recipe for my entire life, so it is a dish that holds many special memories for me.
I hope you enjoy it too!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 to 1 bag of tortellini
  • Pre-sliced ham (amount may vary)
  • Butter (amount may vary)
  • Heavy Cream (amount may vary)
  • Parmesan Cheese (amount may vary)

Instructions

  1. Fill a large pot with water and place on stove
  2. Chop ham into small pieces and set aside
  3. Grate parmesan cheese for sauce and set aside
  4. Bring water to a boil and add tortellini
  5. Combine your cheese, heavy cream, and butter in a separate pot and stir until sauce forms
  6. Once noodles are cooked, place them in a large bowl and pour alfredo sauce on top
  7. Add in your ham and mix
  8. Enjoy!
